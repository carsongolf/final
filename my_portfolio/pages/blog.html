The basis of this theory relies on conflict, specifically conflict being the main turning point for psychological development. When a person goes through a great deal of pain and anguish, they commonly find a dramatic change in their mental state and infrastructure. This also applies on the opposite spectrum if a person fails to overcome a conflict or trial.
